Conan Doyle was born on May 22nd 1859

In 1876, Conan Doyle began his medical studies at the University of Edinburgh

On October 22nd 1881, Conan Doyle graduated and enlisted as a doctor aboard a steamer to Western Africa

In 1885, Conan Doyle married Louisa Hawkins

In 1887, Conan Doyle wrote his first Sherlock Holmes adventure, A Study in Scarlet
